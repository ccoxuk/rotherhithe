## Appendix 13.21 — Sample MoU (non-legal) — Template

**[GOVERNANCE NOTE: LEGAL STANDING]**
**An MoU is generally not legally binding.**
It records intent, not obligation. Do not use an MoU for financial transactions or critical safety liabilities.
**Use For:** Collaboration, shared goals, in-kind support.
**Do Not Use For:** Construction contracts, leases, employment.

Purpose: A simple, short-form template designed for low-complexity partner arrangements (community groups, local businesses) where a full contract is not proportionate.

MoU: [Party A: Project name] and [Party B: Community Organisation / Business]
- Purpose / Summary: [Short statement of what the parties will deliver together]
- Duration: [Start Date] – [End Date]
- Deliverables: [Party A provides X; Party B provides Y] (e.g., equipment, volunteers, space, promotion)
- Safeguarding: [Party B confirms it will carry out DBS checks as required and comply with the project safeguarding policy].
- Insurance: Each party will maintain adequate insurance for their liabilities; the Project holds public liability insurance.
- Confidentiality & GDPR: Parties agree not to share personal data outside the scope of the partnership without consent.
- Termination: Either party may withdraw with 30 days’ notice; immediate withdrawal if policies are breached.
- Signature: Party A signature / Party B signature / Date
