## Appendix 13.15 — RACI Matrix & Decision Log

**[GOVERNANCE NOTE: CLARITY OF COMMAND]**
**Ambiguity causes accidents.**
Everyone on site must know who is in charge of Safety, Finance, and Artistic decisions.
**Rule:** If a task has two "Accountable" people, nobody is accountable.

RACI: Core functions
- Permissions & Planning: Responsible – Producer; Accountable – Director; Consulted – Planner/Architect; Informed – Board
- Health & Safety: Responsible – Principal Contractor / H&S Lead; Accountable – Producer; Consulted – HSE / Asset Protection; Informed – Board
- Finance: Responsible – Finance Lead; Accountable – Director/Board CFO; Consulted – Funders; Informed – Producer

Decision Log template
- Decision ID | Date | Decision Title | Decision Maker(s) | Rationale | Evidence | Action Taken | Outcome / Follow-up
