**Budget Control Strategy:**

1. **Secure structural costs first** - Biggest impact; hardest to reduce mid-project
2. **Get multiple professional fee quotes** - Wide variation between providers
3. **Lock in long-lead items early** - Canopies, generators have 6-8 week lead times
4. **Maintain 15-20% contingency** - Based on High case scenario for top 3 cost drivers
5. **Monitor actuals weekly** - Variance >10% on high-sensitivity items triggers review

**Funding Strategy Implication:**  
Secure funding for **Likely case + 15% contingency**. If High case materializes on multiple items simultaneously, you may need to:
- Activate reserve funding sources
- Reduce scope (shorter run, smaller capacity)
- Seek additional grants (requires 3-6 month lead time)
