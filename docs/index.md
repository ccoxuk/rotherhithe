# Rotherhithe Theatre Project Manual

<div class="hero-section" markdown="1">

## From Conception to Closure

**A comprehensive guide to developing temporary and permanent theatre venues in complex urban environments.**

This manual provides rigorous, end-to-end governance and delivery frameworks for establishing theatre projects in high-constraint settings—specifically calibrated for railway-adjacent sites in Southeast London, but applicable to any capital arts venue development.

</div>

---

## What You'll Find Here

### 1. Getting Started
Strategic overview, 7-day quick-start playbook, and guidance on using this manual effectively.

### 2. Project Delivery Framework  
Three-phase lifecycle (**Discovery → Implementation → Closure**) with decision gates, risk management, and governance structures.

### 3. Specialist Guidance
Critical topics for railway-adjacent venues: Asset Protection Agreements, planning permissions, CDM compliance, budget sensitivity, and partnership development.

### 4. Toolkit & Templates  
25+ ready-to-use resources: risk registers, H&S checklists, funder reports, legal templates (MoU, DPA), GANTT guides, and accessibility frameworks.

### 5. Resources & References
Statutory contacts, sector organizations, glossary of technical terms, and bibliography of regulatory sources.

---

## How to Navigate

**For First-Time Users:** Read sequentially from Getting Started through Project Phases to build foundational knowledge.

**For Active Projects:** Jump directly to relevant phase sections (Discovery/Implementation/Closure) and use the Toolkit for operational templates.

**For Specialists:** Use the navigation menu to access technical appendices on rail safety, legal compliance, or financial modelling.

Each section includes:
- **Golden thread commentary** explaining the "why" behind processes  
- **Epistemic markers** showing evidence basis (statutory requirements, good practice, inferred judgement)
- **Cross-references** to related templates and guidance
- **Decision frameworks** for go/no-go evaluation
